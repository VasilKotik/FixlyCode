Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. No Tracking & No Analytics

We respect your privacy.

We do NOT track you. FixlyCode does not use Google Analytics, cookies for tracking, or any third-party behavioral monitoring tools. We do not collect your IP address for marketing purposes.

2. Data Handling

  • No Account Required: You can use the full functionality of FixlyCode without registering.
  • Local Storage: Your chat history, preferences (dark mode), and saved snippets are stored locally on your device using browser LocalStorage. We do not have access to this data.
  • AI Processing: When you run code, it is sent securely to our AI providers (Google Gemini, OpenRouter) strictly for generating the response.

3. Contact Us

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not send marketing emails. If you have any questions or find a bug, please contact us via our GitHub Issues page.
