About FixlyCode

Empowering developers with AI since 2025

Our Mission

FixlyCode was built with a simple goal: to make AI coding assistance accessible to everyone, for free. We believe that debugging shouldn't be a headache. Whether you are a student learning Python or a senior developer optimizing a React component, FixlyCode acts as your intelligent pair programmer.

How It Works

Unlike traditional tools, FixlyCode leverages multiple cutting-edge AI models to analyze your code. We integrate with:

  • Google Gemini 2.5 Flash: For lightning-fast reasoning and logic analysis.
  • OpenRouter Models: Including Qwen, DeepSeek, and Grok for diverse coding perspectives.

Privacy First

We respect your code. FixlyCode operates with a privacy-first approach. We do not require you to create an account, and your chat history is stored locally on your device via LocalStorage, not on our servers.

Open Source

Transparency is key. FixlyCode is an open-source project. Developers can audit our code, contribute features, or report issues directly on our GitHub repository.
