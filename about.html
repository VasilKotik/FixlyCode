About FixlyCode

Open Source AI Assistant for Developers

Our Mission

FixlyCode is a free tool designed to help developers debug, optimize, and understand code instantly. Hosted on fixlycode.com, we provide a fast and private alternative to heavy coding assistants.

How It Works

We combine the power of multiple AI models (Google Gemini & OpenRouter) into a simple, user-friendly interface. No login required, no complex setup.

Open Source & Community

We believe in transparency. FixlyCode is open source, meaning anyone can inspect our code, verify our privacy claims, or contribute to the project.
